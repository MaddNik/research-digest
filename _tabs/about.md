This is a personal site for tracking research across five fields:

- **Photonics**: integrated and silicon photonics, photonic computing, optical interconnects
- **Post-Quantum Cryptography**: lattice, code, hash, and isogeny-based schemes, standardization efforts
- **Fully Homomorphic Encryption**: schemes, accelerators, libraries, and applications
- **Cryptography**: protocols, primitives, cryptanalysis, zero-knowledge proofs
- **Hardware Security**: side-channel attacks, fault injection, secure hardware, trusted execution environments

---

**Papers** are added weekly. Each paper gets a short abstract-level summary visible directly on the card. Click the title or the Read more button to open a longer write-up in a panel without leaving the page. Each topic has its own tab, or use the Tags tab to browse across fields by keyword.

**Deep Summaries** are longer, structured write-ups on individual papers - covering motivation, methods, results, and significance in depth. Find them under the Deep Summaries tab.

**Conferences** tracks upcoming events in semiconductor, photonics, and cryptography research: location, focus area, dates, and submission or registration links where available. Updated monthly.

**My Research** is a personal reading list. Use the bookmark and save buttons on any card to track papers you want to read or have saved.

---

> Paper summaries are generated automatically from abstracts and may contain errors or omissions. Always read the linked source before citing or building on a result.
