**Frontier Research Digest** is an automatically curated weekly roundup of new
research papers across five fields:

- **Photonics** — integrated/silicon photonics, photonic computing, optical interconnects
- **PQC** — post-quantum cryptography, lattice/code/hash/isogeny-based schemes, standardization
- **FHE** — fully homomorphic encryption, accelerators, libraries, applications
- **Cryptography** — protocols, primitives, cryptanalysis, zero-knowledge
- **Hardware security** — side channels, fault attacks, secure hardware, trusted execution

A scheduled agent searches preprint servers and publication databases every week,
verifies each summary against its source, and publishes a new post here **every
Friday**. Each entry has a title, a short verified summary, the venue/date, and a
direct link to the paper.

> This site is generated automatically. Summaries are AI-written from paper
> abstracts and may contain errors — always check the linked source.
