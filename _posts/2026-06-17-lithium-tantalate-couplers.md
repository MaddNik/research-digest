description: "3D-printed polymer couplers give octave-spanning, sub-decibel fiber-to-chip coupling for thin-film lithium tantalate waveguides."
---

The authors fabricate out-of-plane polymer coupling interfaces using 3D direct
laser writing for thin-film lithium tantalate waveguides. They report ultra-low
coupling losses of 0.9 decibels per interface for strip waveguides and 1.25
decibels per interface for rib waveguides. The couplers operate across an octave
of bandwidth spanning wavelengths from 850 nanometers to 1740 nanometers.
Combined with on-chip waveguides that exhibit low propagation loss, the platform
targets high-speed optical signal processing and potential quantum applications.
The work addresses a persistent packaging bottleneck for emerging lithium
tantalate photonic integrated circuits.
