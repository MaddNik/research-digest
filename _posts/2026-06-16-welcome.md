This is a personal site for keeping track of research and conferences in
photonics, post-quantum cryptography (PQC), fully homomorphic encryption (FHE),
cryptography, and hardware security.

New papers are tracked every week. Each paper appears as its own card: click
"Read summary" to open a longer summary in a popup with a direct link to the
paper. Use the topic tabs to browse a single field.

The Conferences tab tracks upcoming semiconductor and photonics events, with
location, focus, dates, and a link when available, refreshed monthly.

> Summaries are generated from paper abstracts and may contain errors. Always
> open the linked source before citing.
