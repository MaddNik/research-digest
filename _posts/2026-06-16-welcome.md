This site publishes an **automatically curated weekly digest** of new research
papers in **photonics, post-quantum cryptography (PQC), fully homomorphic
encryption (FHE), cryptography, and hardware security**.

A scheduled agent runs every **Friday morning**: it searches preprint servers
(arXiv, IACR ePrint) and publication databases for the week's notable papers,
writes a short summary for each, verifies that summary against the source, and
publishes the results here as a new post.

Browse by topic using the **Categories** and **Tags** tabs, or use the search
box. The first full digest will appear this Friday.

> Summaries are generated from paper abstracts and may contain errors. Always
> open the linked source before citing.
