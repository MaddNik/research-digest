title: "Multi-channel high-speed flip-chip packaging platform for thin-film lithium niobate photonic circuits"

byline: "Zhenzheng Wang et al. (arXiv:2606.15391, Jun 13 2026)"
description: "A flip-chip packaging platform delivers dozens of electrical channels to centimeter-scale thin-film lithium niobate chips with 50 gigahertz bandwidth."
---

The authors present a flip-chip packaging platform that addresses the need for
dense, high-speed electrical interfacing of thin-film lithium niobate photonic
circuits. The platform delivers 13 high-speed and 32 low-speed signals to a
centimeter-sized chip with low bonding loss and low inter-channel crosstalk up to
50 gigahertz bandwidth. Two demonstration circuits are presented to validate the
approach. A 2 by 8 optical switch achieves roughly 3 decibels of insertion loss
with a 34 picosecond switching time. An electro-optic comb-based transmitter
shows 50 gigahertz comb generation and 20 gigabits per second data transmission.
