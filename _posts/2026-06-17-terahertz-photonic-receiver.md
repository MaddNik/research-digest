description: "Thin-film lithium niobate terahertz photonic receivers reach link noise performance that surpasses electronic frontends across multiple terahertz windows."
---

The authors demonstrate low-noise integrated terahertz photonic frontends built
on thin-film lithium niobate. The receivers deliver undegraded link noise
performance across three major terahertz windows from 140 to 450 gigahertz. They
achieve effective isotropic noise figures of 13.6 decibels at 250 gigahertz and
16.2 decibels at 450 gigahertz. The systems support multi-link communication at
up to 20 gigabits per second. The work positions integrated photonic receivers as
a route toward terahertz systems for sixth-generation wireless networks where
conventional electronic frontends struggle.
