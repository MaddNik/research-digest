{%- comment -%} Per-visitor Minecraft skin. Fully client-side (static site, no backend). A random seed is stored in localStorage on first visit, so the same device always sees the same skin while different visitors get different ones. The seed is hashed to pick a username from a curated list; the skin image is rendered by a third-party skin service, with a fallback chain then hide.
